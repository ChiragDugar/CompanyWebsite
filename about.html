Our History

Established 5 decades ago, Shree Sanitations is a reputed company engaged in maufacture and dealership of a complete range of Bath Fittings in Tamil Nadu.We offer a wide range of products like Sanitarywares, Bath Faucets, Bathroom Accessories, Pipes, Kitchen Sinks and Acrylic Accesories form brands like Corsa, Orange, Metro, Waterman, Diamond, Nirali, Jaquar, Parryware, Roca and Hindware

Since our establishment, we have been expanding at an exponential rate. From dealing with a limited number of brands and products, to a wide range of brands, we have come a long way. Our aim is to provide the best quality and performance to the customers. We want to provide the best products at a very reasonable rate.

We want to make sure that all the customers are satisfied with our service. With a highly dedicated technical and the management team, we give our best for our customers.
